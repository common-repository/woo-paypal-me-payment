Use Paypal.Me as payment method in your woocommerce shop.

== Description ==

Sometimes you need to recieve privat payments.

So use this Paypal.me plugin with your Paypal.me url and show in addition your Paypal E-mail Adress to recieve payments.

This plugin redirect your customer after some seconds to your Paypal.me url. After you recieved an payment, you have to change the order status by your own.

Notice: Woocommerce have to be installed and active.

* Load the Plugin in your Plugins-Dir. or use the plugin installer.
* Activate your Plugin
